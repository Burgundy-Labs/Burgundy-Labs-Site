About Burgundy Labs

Burgundy Labs is a small software oriented company founded in Houghton, Michigan. Through the discovery of various software needs on the campus of Michigan Technological University, several students began to address the problems themselves. Our first project, TimeSlot aims to improve the experience students and tutors have when scheduling appointments, and working at learning centers on campuses around the world.

Our focus is on improving the lives of our users through thoughtful design, user testing, and high quality development. We believe that identifying needs and addressing them through effective software solutions will allow us to continue to grow, and provide the great user experience we strive for.

Our Projects

TimeSlot connects students with learning center tutors in an easy to use scheduling web app. Tutors are able to set their available times, students are able to schedule appointments, and administrators can view report data. TimeSlot connects these three user groups at all angles by using tailored user experiences, modern design practices, and human factors psychology.
